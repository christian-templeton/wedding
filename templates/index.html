CHRISTIAN + KELLY TOGETHER AT LAST

September 21, 2019 -- Marin, California

Part 1: The First Date

There are various accounts. As told by Christian, their first date was at the Academy of Sciences, which Kelly contends was not a date.

Christian and Kelly worked together at the time, so Christian "bumped into" Kelly and asked if she wanted to get lunch the following week. This too, was not a date. During the not-a-date lunch, Christian asked Kelly to go to Wilco at the Greek Theater. She said yes!

Part 2: The Proposal

After six years of being together, Kelly curiously developed a shared interest in cycling, one of the things Christian loves. Christian and Kelly trained for a 65-mile charity ride in Napa, CA and the stage was set. After 55 miles Christian feigned a flat tire. Christian, Kelly and her parents who were on the ride pulled over. Instead of a tube, Christian pulled out a ring and popped the question. It was kind of like this... but in spandex!

After Kelly said yes, her sister Julie jumped out from her nearby bunker with flowers and Champagne. Christian carried the Champagne in his bike jersey the remaining 10 miles and sprayed Kelly as she passed the finish line!

Part 3: The Wedding
